Formatting:

Table of Contents (18 pts)
Chapter headings (12 pts)
Sub Headings (11 pts)


Chapter Heading (18 pts)
example:- 1. Introduction 
	
Sub-Heading (14 pts)
example:- 1.1 Purpose

Text (11 pts)

-> Text should be justified
-> Figures should be captioned
-> All figures must be mentioned in the text (a "callout") by their number.
